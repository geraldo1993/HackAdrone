Hackadrone

Hackadrone is a large competition between groups of attendees which usually takes between 15-34 hours. Students are trying to solve different real life problems. At the beginning of the event we will present students with details surrounding how the Hackathon will precede and what their problems should consist of. The event will be hosted by MLH (Major League Hacking) and we plan to provide professors, and experienced specialists as mentors alongside a numbers of small conferences and presentations for the students to enjoy.

What is hackton?

A Hackathon is a large competition between groups of attendees which usually takes between 15-34 hours. Students are trying to solve different real life problems.

What is a drone?

Drones are Unmanned Aerial Vehicles UAV’s which fly either remotely controlled or fitted into software. We are trying to develpe better controll over drones.
